Welcome to Our Documentation Hub!¶

If you are new to DeepLabCut, we have 3 resources we recommend before jumping in. (1) Please first read at the Nature Protocol paper; (2) Check out the quick video on navigating the docs; (3) Check out the free DeepLabCut Course: we have put together a “course” on the science of DeepLabCut and how to use it.

Otherwise, here are the links to all the key steps to get you up and running within a day!

  • How to install DeepLabCut

  • Decide on your needs: there are two main modes, standard DeepLabCut or multi-animal DeepLabCut. We highly recommend carefully considering which one is best for your needs. For example, a white mouse + black mouse would call for standard, while two black mice would use multi-animal.
